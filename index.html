461 Anderson — Garage Network Rack NavePoint 6U Install · Plywood Backer · For Domenic + electrician

All low-voltage gear lives in one wall-mounted cabinet on a plywood backer in the garage. This page shows the layout, how the cabinet attaches to the wall, and what equipment goes in which rack unit.

Elevation — wall section

Cabinet centered on plywood. Plywood lag-bolted to studs at 8 points (16" o.c.). Bottom of cabinet at 48" AFF — keeps PDU off the slab, leaves room for service loop.

Install summary

One 4'×4' × 3/4" plywood backer, sealed and painted to match wall. Lag through to studs at 16" o.c. — minimum 8 lags (2 per stud, top and bottom). Bottom of plywood at ~12" AFF, bottom of cabinet at ~48" AFF.

What the electrician owns

  • Dedicated 20A circuit for the rack — single duplex receptacle below cabinet, not shared with garage outlets.
  • Low-voltage stub-up: 1× 1.5" ENT (smurf tube) from this location to the attic, for all house CAT6 home runs to land at the patch panel.
  • Sonic fiber entry: separate 3/4" conduit from exterior demarc to bottom of cabinet for the ONT fiber jumper.
  • Bonding: #6 ground from cabinet ground lug to nearest panel ground (UDM-SE chassis bonds through PDU).
  • No shared neutrals on the rack circuit.

What's pre-purchased (do not buy)

  • NavePoint 6U cabinet, 1U vented shelf, CyberPower PDU, TRENDnet patch panel, UDM-SE, WD Purple 8TB, U-POE-at injector, (3) U7 Pro XG APs.
  • Ray supplies all CAT6, keystones, and patch cables. Electrician home-runs CAT6 from each AP / wall jack to this cabinet — leave 10' of slack at the rack end, coiled in U2.

Wire-up flow — for the electrician

  1. Sonic fiber → ONT (on U6 shelf). ONT Ethernet out → UDM-SE WAN port.
  2. Each house CAT6 run → punched down on U3 patch panel keystone. Label every port (room + position).
  3. Short patch cables (Ray supplies) from patch panel ports up to UDM-SE PoE+ ports for APs and cameras.
  4. All AC: every device's power cord plugs into the U1 PDU. PDU plugs into the dedicated 20A wall receptacle.
  5. 3rd AP (longest run): patch panel port → U-POE-at injector data-in, injector data+power-out → that AP. Injector wall-wart plugs into the PDU.
